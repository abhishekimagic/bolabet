RESPONSIBLE GAMBLING << Back

Bolabet.com strives to create a responsible and humble gaming environment for each and everyone playing at Bolabet.com. To most people gaming is socially enjoyable and a harmless source of entertainment. But, sometimes it just becomes too much and a small number of players just can't say no to that extra spin on the slots or that extra bet.

Know your limits!

At Bolabet.com our internal system includes a global deposit limit which is active for all players and set by our payment department. This limit is independent of those set by the various payment merchants available on our deposit pages.

This limit includes:

Deposit Limit

  • 24 Hour Deposit Limit - You decide what is the limit of the funds you can deposit within 24 hours.
  • Weekly Deposit Limit - You decide what is the limit of the funds you can deposit within one week.
  • Montly Deposit Limit - You decide what is the limit of the funds you can deposit within one month.

Bet Limit

  • 24 Hour Bet Limit - You decide what is the limit of the funds you can bet within 24 hours.
  • Weekly Bet Limit - You decide what is the limit of the funds you can bet within one week.
  • Monthly Bet Limit - You decide what is the limit of the funds you can bet within one month.

Loss Limit

  • 24 Hour Loss Limit - You decide what is the limit of the funds you can loose within 24 hours.
  • Weekly Loss Limit - You decide what is the limit of the funds you can loose within one week.
  • Monthly Loss Limit - You decide what is the limit of the funds you can loose within one month

Loss and Bet limits are checked every time you log on the web site. If your losses or bets are above the limit, you will not be able to place a bet until the time period limit applies to is over.

During this time, you can withdraw funds and access the web site.

Self Exclusion

At Bolabet.com you can also exclude yourself from our real money games for a specific period of time. Self exclusion will prevent you from placing sports bets, casino bets or placinga deposit. You can only withdraw funds.

You can choose between:

  • One Day Self Exclusion
  • One Week Self Exclusion
  • One Month Self Exclusion
  • Custom Time Period Self Exclusion
  • Permanent closure of your account

Within this period you will not receive any promotional materials or adverts from us. All exclusion options except Permanent account closure are available on Personal Limits Page under Your Account settings.If you wish to close your account permanently, please contact our

support: manager@bolabet.com
