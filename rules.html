Rules

The expressions used in these Betting Rules on sports Events and the other Events (hereinafter "Rules") during betting organizing on sports events and the other events, have the following meaning :

BOOKMAKER: Legal or individual person who organize Betting on sports events and the other events on the behalf of Company;

EVENT: The Event is every future uncertain competition with measurable outcome ;

BETTING: Investing, deposit money on some predicted outcome of Event , in which we can organize Betting in accordance with Law; Betting consists of accepting the Stake from the Customer under the conditions which Company suggests.

OFFER: The list of Events and theirs outcome with odds, which the Company suggests for Betting.

CUSTOMER: Participant in Betting, the individual who accepted these Rules, fulfils conditions for participating and with investing money takes part in Betting;

STAKE: Deposit, invested money in domestic currency which Customer pays to predict Outcome of Event

OUTCOME: The result of Event for which according to Law, the Company organizes Betting and determines odds.

TOTAL RETURN: Total profit of Customers as a result of Betting, including the Stake;

PURE PROFIT - earning: Sum which the Customer earns by Betting: TOTAL RETURN minus STAKE

PALPABLE ERROR: Each mistake in Offer in connection with the exact Event ,concerning odds, participants, time and place of match, impossibility to check result accuracy, printing errors in Offer or any other reason for which Bookmaker estimate that affected on Betting regularity;

DAILY RETURN: It is profit on tickets which are accepted or paid on the same day ;

ANTEPOST BETTING: It is betting on the Events which will be played after more than 15 days from the day when the Customer placed bets;

REFUND: Returning the Stake to the Customer ,in case that Betting was not valid at any reason;

VOID BETTING: The situation when instead of already written odd on ticket, it counts odd 1 (for example: postponed match, cancelled match, incorrect event.)

ASSOCIATED BETTING (syndicate): The series of the same or similar placed bets in the same day in one or more Bet Shops by more Customers in which Bookmaker suspect that they are joined to avoid limitations established in this Rules;

ODD: Number which is multiplied with the Stake to calculate potential Total Return;

FAVORITE: Team or player that according to Company has bigger chance to win (odd on favorite’s win is smaller );

OUTSIDER: Team or player that according to Company has smaller chance to win (odd on outsider’s win is bigger);

HOME: Team or Player which is first listed in the Offer (mark 1.)-it do not need to be actually host of that Event;

AWAY: Team or Player which is second listed in the Offer (mark 2.)- it do not need to be actually guest of that Event;

TOURNAMENT: Competition-championship involving a large number of competitors, all participating in battle for the best placement;

DIVIDED ODDS: Number which is multiplied with direct Odds to calculate payment for the placement, it is related only to tournament betting ;

PLACEMENT: It is related only to Betting on Tournaments, where number of valid placements is declared by Bookmaker (for example: first, second, third place),and the Customer profits if his selection won placement he was betting for and which Bookmaker predicted for profit.

HANDICAP: Advantage given to the outsider, to equal possible Outcome of Event and to increase value of Betting. Handicap is always added to achieve result first listed team/competition.

OFFICIAL INFORMING SOURCES:

1. Official website of the organizer

2. Official website of the host

4. Official site of the away team. Livescore internet sites are not official and relevant during settling the Events.
