How to play

It is so easy to get started playing with Bolabet!

Step 1: JOIN NOW & REGISTER...

First, you need to JOIN NOW by REGISTERING your details...just click on the button towards the top right of the screen...

Just enter your 10-digit phone number (this will be your username) and also choose a password...

Remember, it's very important to choose a password that meets our high security requirements... It must containt at least one CAPITAL letter, one small letter, 1 number & a symbol like this!

This is to protect your account with the strongest security so nobody else can get inside and steal your funds...

Step 2: DEPOSIT cash...

After you have registered, the next stage on the journey to becoming a Bolabet player is to DEPOSIT.

To do this you need to click on the DEPOSIT NOW button...

You will see that Bolabet offers the widest range of deposit options...

These include:

MTN Mobile Money

Airtel Mobile Money

Touch 4 Pay

543 Konse Konse

Bank Transfer.

Step 3: Place your BETS...

Next is the exciting part...Now you must choose your bets by adding the teams or events you want to bet on to the Betslip...

TIPS & TRICKS:

Use the navigation menu on the left of your screen to choose the sport you are interested in - for example football.

Then scroll down the list to find the games you are interested in.

You can either search by country (for example England for the English Premier League) or look at all the games that you are interested in.

Once you have found the game that interests you, you must decide what you want to bet on.

The most popular markets are typically which team will win in 90 minutes (Home team, away team, or the draw) but there are typically hundreds of other markets for every game. Will both teams score (GG) ?

How many goals will there be?

The choice is nearly endless.

Once you have decided on your bet you must click on your bet.

This will automatically add the bet to the betslip on the right hand side of the screen.

If you prefer, you can add many events to the same betslip. For example, you could choose six different teams to win and add them all to one betslip.

In this way it becomes harder to win (because all teams must for you to be a winner) but the odds will become huge so you could hit a huge payout for a tiny stake.

If you make a mistake you can click to delete a selection you no longer want on your betslip.

Once you are happy with your betslip it's time to start playing.

You can click on the betslip and now you must choose your stake. The minimum stake is just 3 ZMW but you can bet any amount up to the total balance you have deposited in your account.(subject to certain restrictions on very large bets).

If at any time you want to see how much money is in your account, just look at the top bar of the screen or click on wallet to see your balance.

Once you are happy with your bet and stake you just click "place bet" to enter your bet and you will receive a confirmation on the screen.

Later, you can log back into your account to check the result of the game and see if your bet was a winner or loser.
